# Q3 Planning

The third quarter focuses on stabilizing the billing platform and expanding the reporting product. Engineering will spend the first six weeks on reliability work before starting new feature development.

## Objectives

- Reduce billing incident rate by half compared to Q2.
- Ship the new reporting export flow to all teams.
- Complete the migration off the legacy billing service.

## Headcount plan

| Team               | Q2 | Q3 | Change |
|--------------------|----|----|--------|
| Payments Platform  | 8  | 10 | +2     |
| Reporting          | 5  | 7  | +2     |
| Platform Infra     | 6  | 6  | 0      |
| Data               | 4  | 5  | +1     |

## Key dates

Planning review happens in the second week of the quarter. The mid-quarter checkpoint is scheduled for the seventh week. Budget sign off is required before any new hire is opened.

## Risks

The largest risk is the billing migration slipping, which would block the reporting export work that depends on the new billing events. The Payments Platform team owns the migration and the sequencing.
