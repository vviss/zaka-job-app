# Services Directory

This document lists ownership for services in the payments area. Ownership means the team responsible for on call, deployments, and long term maintenance.

- Unified Billing: owned by the Payments Platform team.
- LegacyBilling: owned by the Data team, which maintains it for historical invoice lookups until shutdown.
- Reporting Export: owned by the Reporting team.
- Metering Gateway: owned by the Platform Infra team.

Ownership is reviewed each quarter. If a service is deprecated, its owner is responsible for the shutdown plan even after traffic stops.
