# Team Handbook

This handbook covers the working norms for the Acme engineering teams.

Working hours are flexible, with a shared core window from late morning to early afternoon for meetings. Code review is required before merge, and every change needs at least one approval.

The annual planning deadline is October 1. All teams must submit their roadmap for the following year before this date so that budget can be finalized. This date was moved earlier this year to align with the new fiscal calendar.

On call rotations run weekly. The person on call is the first responder for incidents in their team's services and is expected to acknowledge alerts within fifteen minutes during working hours.
