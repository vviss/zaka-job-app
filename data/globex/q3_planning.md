# Q3 Planning

Globex is focused this quarter on launching the customer portal and reducing support load. Engineering will prioritize the portal ahead of internal tooling work.

## Objectives

- Launch the self service customer portal to all regions.
- Cut inbound support tickets by a quarter through better self service.
- Finish the identity provider upgrade.

## Key dates

The planning review is in the first week. The launch target for the portal is the end of the quarter. The annual planning deadline for next year is September 30.
