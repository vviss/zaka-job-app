# Data Handling Policy

This policy covers how internal systems store and retain operational data.

Application logs are retained for 400 days before automatic deletion. This retention window was chosen to cover the full annual audit cycle plus a buffer, and it applies to all production services without exception. Requests for longer retention must be approved by the security lead in writing.

Personal data is stored only in the systems listed in the data inventory. Backups are encrypted at rest and are held for 90 days. Access to raw logs is limited to the owning team and the platform on call rotation.
